## Tier System

| Tier | Stars | Max HYPE in Buy Window |
|------|-------|------------------------|
| 1    | ⭐    | 1 HYPE                 |
| 2    | ⭐⭐  | 2 HYPE                 |
| 3    | ⭐⭐⭐ | 3 HYPE                |

## Staking Rules

### Early Buyer Vault
- Opens: During the 1-hour exclusive buy window
- Closes: At the end of hour 1 (permanent)
- Reward: Share of 10% of total supply over 3 weeks
- Unstaking: Permanent exit, no re-entry, accumulation forfeited

### Post-Launch Vault
- Opens: When public trading begins
- Reward: Share of 5% of total supply over 3 weeks
- Unstaking: Permanent exit, no re-entry, accumulation forfeited
