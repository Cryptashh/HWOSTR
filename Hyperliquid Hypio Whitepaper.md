## 1. Vision

$HYPIO is the economic asset of the HYPIO NFT collection, deployed on Hyperliquid. It is not designed to reach a speculative market cap and dump. It is designed for longevity — driving consistent volume, rewarding long-term holders, and building one of the most active communities the space has seen.

The success of the token depends on collective participation. The more holders play the game (holding, staking, and engaging in events), the stronger the ecosystem becomes for everyone.

## 2. The Three Pillars

### 2.1 An Appreciating NFT
HYPIO NFTs benefit directly from the token economy. The 5% transaction tax is entirely dedicated to buybacks and strategic bids on HYPIO NFTs, creating persistent buy pressure at the floor.

### 2.2 A Token With Real Utility
$HYPIO is not a meme. It powers access to staking vaults, community events, partner spotlights, games, and future mechanisms within the HYPIO ecosystem. The more tokens you hold, the greater your position within every event and every layer of the economy.

### 2.3 A Mechanism Yet to Be Revealed
A third mechanism — not yet disclosed — will allow holders to accumulate additional $HYPE. Details will be revealed in time, at the right moment.

## 3. Launch Mechanics

### 3.1 Tier System & Buy Window
Holders are divided into tiers based on their HYPIO NFT holdings, measured in stars (1 to 4).

- 1 star = access to buy up to 1 HYPE during the exclusive window
- 2 stars = access to buy up to 2 HYPE
- 3 stars = access to buy up to 3 HYPE
- 4 stars = access to buy up to 4 HYPE

**Important:** No single transaction can exceed 1 HYPE, regardless of tier. A 4-star holder executes up to 4 separate transactions of 1 HYPE each. This is the anti-whale mechanism by design.

### 3.2 The 1-Hour Exclusive Window
Before public trading opens, there is a 1-hour exclusive buy window for tier 1 to 4 holders only.

- No selling is permitted during this window.
- Every buyer is locked in together, building the floor collectively.
- Staking vault access closes at the end of this hour. After that, it is permanently closed to new entrants.

The date of the window will be announced shortly.

### 3.3 Public Trading
After the first hour, public buying and selling opens. At this point, the early buyer staking vault is closed to new participants. Public buyers enter a market where early holders have already established a strong floor together.

---

## 4. Why Hold During Hour 1?

Early buyers who hold together during the exclusive window are the only participants in the market for 60 minutes. No sellers. Only accumulation. This dynamic naturally supports a strong floor for when public trading opens.

Selling immediately forfeits staking rights and accumulated rewards. Holding rewards patience with free token distribution over 3 weeks.

## 5. Economy & Sustainability

The $HYPIO token economy is built on a flywheel designed for long-term activity, not speculation.

### 5.1 The 5% Tax Flywheel
Every transaction and transfer carries a 5% tax.
100% of this tax is dedicated to:
- Buybacks of HYPIO NFTs at the floor
- Strategic bids placed by a designated expert

This is executed manually, not by bots. The expert actively monitors and responds to market conditions — fighting manipulators, jeeters, and snipers in real time. All activity is visible live on the HWOSTR dashboard.

### 5.2 Volume-Driven Sweeps
Rather than chasing a market cap number, $HYPIO is built to drive real, consistent volume. Periodic sweeps are part of the long-term strategy, rewarding patient holders over time.

## 6. Staking

### 6.1 Early Buyer Staking Vault
Holders who buy during the first hour and stake before the vault closes will share 10% of the total $HYPIO supply, distributed over 3 weeks.

- Unstaking at any point means permanently losing your accumulated share.
- There is no re-entry into this vault after it closes.

### 6.2 Post-Launch Staking
A separate staking vault will distribute 5% of supply to stakers once public trading opens.

Same rule applies: unstaking is permanent. You exit the distribution and cannot re-enter.
